Our app translates text automatically to the viewers's native language and then returns the text to the reader. This allows simple and open communication without language barrier limitations.
       
       

Our Mission

       

Our goal is creating an application that bridges the language barrier and allows others to converse freely.

We came up with the idea because it was something he struggled with one day in his community. He was playing the piano in a public park and struggled to converse with an aspiring pianist due to the language barrier. The two got through it with our separate translating apps, however, he wondered if there was a more efficient and beneficial alternative. Everyone has been in a scenario in which they wished that communicating with a speaker of another language was easier. When you travel abroad, it is nice to know that you will be able to communicate with those around you. Even in the US, there many people from many different backgrounds, and communication in other tongues can be a big assset.
